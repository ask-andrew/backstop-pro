Why this exists

I spent years as a CSM and leading teams of CSMs. We were organized, diligent, and genuinely cared about our clients. But threads still slipped — not because anyone was careless, but because Gmail was never built to track the silence after you hit send.

When you manage ten people, a missed reply isn't just awkward. It becomes a client escalating to your VP because their CSM "never got back to them." The email was there. The intention was there. The follow-up just never happened.

I looked for a tool that would watch a thread, draft a nudge if they went quiet, and ask me before sending anything — lightweight enough for a single important email, not a CRM migration project.

It didn't exist. So I built it.
