🚀 Backstop Beta

Installation Guide for Testers

1
Download

Get the beta bundle and extract it to your Desktop.

⬇ Download Backstop_Beta.zip
2
Load Extension

Go to chrome://extensions, enable Developer Mode, and click Load Unpacked. Select the extracted folder.

⚠️ Google Verification

You will see a warning saying Google hasn't verified this app. Click Advanced > Go to Backstop (unsafe). This is normal for beta software!
