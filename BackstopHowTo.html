🚀 Backstop Beta

Installation Guide for Testers

1
Download & Unzip
Download Backstop_Beta.zip and extract it to your Desktop.
2
Open Extensions
In Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions
3
Enable Developer Mode
Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner to ON.
4
Load the Extension
Click Load unpacked and select the extracted dist folder.
5
Open Gmail
Refresh your Gmail tab. You should see the emerald-green Backstop sidebar on the right! 🎉
⚠️ Expected Security Warning

When you first click "Sign in with Google", you'll see: "Google hasn't verified this app."

This is normal! To proceed:

  1. Click "Advanced"
  2. Then click "Go to Backstop (unsafe)"

This happens because the app is in development mode. It's safe to proceed!

📋 What to Test
  • First Look: Does the sidebar appear immediately, or do you need to refresh?
  • I Ain't Afraid of No Ghosts: Does the "Ghost Hunt" tab accurately show your unanswered emails? Did you tinker with the automated messages?
  • Useful Check: Is this helpful? Would this save you time? Do you think this would help others?
  • 2.0: What would make this better?
