🚀 Backstop Beta

Follow these steps to get up and running.

1
Download & Unzip

Get the latest beta bundle:

⬇ Download Backstop_Beta.zip

Once downloaded, extract the ZIP to a permanent location like your Documents or Desktop folder.

2
Load into Chrome

Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions.

Enable Developer Mode in the top-right corner, then click Load Unpacked. Select the folder you just extracted.

⚠️ Google Verification

You will see a warning saying Google hasn't verified this app. Click Advanced > Go to Backstop (unsafe). This is expected behavior for software in private beta!
